Is it free to post a job on Ignition?

All jobs on Ignition can be posted for free

What information is saved in a job template?

The details of the job ad

How do I edit a job template?

After a saving a job as a job template, all edits and updates needed should be done directly on the job that was saved as a job template.

What can I do to help my job posting become visible on Ignition?

In order to provide the best possible job search experience for job seekers, Indeed verifies basic details about a company before advertising its jobs. A physical location (even for work-from-home roles) and a working phone number, for example, are important details that help us provide verifiable opportunities to candidates.

How does Indeed help me with my hiring after I have posted my job?

When creating your job post, you can specify questions to help you screen applicants.* Ask candidates about experience, certifications, or other job requirements when they submit their application. You can also filter applicants based on specific skills, education, experience, and more. Your dashboard also allows you to hide unqualified candidates automatically.
